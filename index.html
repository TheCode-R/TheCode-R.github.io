What We Do

We design analytics dashboards that founders and leadership teams rely on to run the business — not just review numbers.

  • Unified dashboards across marketing, revenue, and operations

  • Automated reporting with a single source of truth

Problems We Solve

Most analytics challenges are not technical — they are structural.

  • Data scattered across Shopify, ad platforms, logistics, and finance
  • Different teams trusting different numbers
  • Manual Excel reporting consuming leadership time
  • Dashboards built but not adopted or trusted
  • Decision-making driven by instinct instead of clarity

How We Work

Our approach is deliberately simple, senior-led, and outcome-driven.

  1. Define Decisions First
    We start by understanding the decisions founders and leaders need to make — not the data available.
  2. Align on Metrics
    We agree on a small set of metrics that truly matter to the business.
  3. Unify the Data
    We bring all critical data into a single, reliable model.
  4. Design for Adoption
    Dashboards are built to be used, trusted, and revisited regularly.
